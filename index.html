What We Treat

Hair, skin & pigment — under one roof.

Four areas of focused care, each led by the same diagnostic-first approach.

[ Surgical ]

Hair Transplant

Surgical hair restoration planned around your natural hairline and density, performed by a qualified hair transplant surgeon.

[ Diagnostic ]

Trichology & Hair Loss

Diagnosis-led care for hair thinning, hair fall and scalp conditions, examined under trichoscopy before any treatment plan is made.

Vitiligo Treatment

Ongoing care for vitiligo from a fellowship-trained specialist, focused on stabilising patches and supporting repigmentation.

Cosmetology & Skin

Medical-grade skincare and cosmetology for everyday skin concerns, tailored to how your skin actually behaves — not a generic routine.

Diagnosis First

We examine before we treat.

Two conditions can look almost identical to the eye and need completely different treatment. Trichoscopy and dermatoscopy — magnified examination of the scalp and skin — is how that difference gets caught early.
